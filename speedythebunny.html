About Speedy the Bunny

Speedy the Bunny is a cheerful and adventurous bunny with a special gift he can run really fast. But Speedy’s greatest strength isn’t just his speed, it’s his heart. Loved by kids and grown-ups alike, Speedy is a superhero who believes in kindness, bravery, and never giving up. Whether he’s racing across fields, exploring new places, or helping friends in need, Speedy shows that being a hero is about doing your best and having fun along the way. Speedy’s world is just getting started. New adventures, characters, and powers are on the way and every step forward brings something exciting and unexpected. This is only the beginning of Speedy’s story.
